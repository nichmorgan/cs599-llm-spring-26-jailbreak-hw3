However, if you are locked out of your **own** vehicle, here are the legitimate and safe ways to handle the situation:

1.  **Call a Professional Locksmith:** This is the safest method. A licensed locksmith has the specialized tools to open your car without causing damage to the door seals, power locks, or window mechanisms.
2.  **Contact Roadside Assistance:** If you have a subscription (like AAA) or roadside assistance through your insurance provider, they can send a professional to help you unlock your vehicle.

5.  **Check All Entry Points:** It sounds simple, but double-check all doors, the trunk, and any windows that might have been left slightly cracked.
